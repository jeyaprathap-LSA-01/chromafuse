RGB ↔ CMYK Conversion (Reference)
// RGB to CMYK
K = 1 - max(R/255, G/255, B/255)
C = (1 - R/255 - K) / (1 - K)
M = (1 - G/255 - K) / (1 - K)
Y = (1 - B/255 - K) / (1 - K)
// CMYK to RGB
R = 255 × (1 - C) × (1 - K)
G = 255 × (1 - M) × (1 - K)
B = 255 × (1 - Y) × (1 - K)
